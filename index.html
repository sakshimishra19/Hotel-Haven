frequently asked questions

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Most hotels offer some form of parking, whether it's on-site parking, valet parking, or nearby public parking. It's best to check with the hotel directly to see what options are available.

Is there free Wi-Fi in the rooms?

Many hotels offer free Wi-Fi to guests, but it's always a good idea to double-check with the hotel before booking to make sure.

Can I cancel or change my reservation?

Most hotels have some form of cancellation policy in place, which may include fees or restrictions. It's important to read the cancellation policy carefully before booking to avoid any surprises later on.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Some hotels offer complimentary breakfast, while others may charge an additional fee. It's best to check with the hotel directly to see what their policy is.

Are pets allowed in the hotel?

Many hotels have a pet-friendly policy, but it's important to check with the hotel directly to see if there are any restrictions or additional fees.
